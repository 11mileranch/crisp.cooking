Bevs Cole Slaw

★★★★★

Prep Time: 25 Cook Time: 5 Total Time: 30 Difficulty: Easy Servings: 6-8 Source: Beverly

Ingredients

1 green cabbage shredded

3 medium carrots grated

2 red apples chopped

1 white onion

1/3 cup mayonnaise

1 tbsp Dijon mustard

1 tbsp lemon juice

1 tbsp sugar

salt and pepper

red wine vinegar

Directions

This salad is refreshing and great to pair with holiday dinners.

Chop or shred the green cabbage. Grate the carrots. Chop the onions thin.
Mix all the ingredients in a large bowl.
Chill and Serve.

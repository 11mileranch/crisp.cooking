Ribeye Steak & Fingerling Potatoes

★★★★

Prep Time: 60 Cook Time: 20 Total Time: 90 Difficulty: Medium Servings: 2

Ingredients

2 Ribeye steaks

1 bag fingerling or smallest potatoes

2 tbsp butter

4 tbsp coconut oil

2 tbsp salt

Directions

Place the steaks on a plate. Pat dry with paper towel. Rub 1 tbsp of salt over both sides of each steak. Let the steaks sit out of the fridge for 45 minutes. Pat dry again before cooking.

Cut the potatoes in half and place cut side down in pre-heated fry pan with coconut oil. Cook on medium-high or high heat until slightly browned. Flip and cook covered with medium heat till done. Check with fork test.

Preheat the air-fryer to 400*. Cook the steaks till desired temp. Use a digital thermometer to check the temp from the side of the fillet.

Flip the steak after 6 minutes and cook for 15-20 minutes total.

Medium-Rare remove the steak at 125*

Cabbage Salad

★★★★★

Cuban Food, Salads

Prep Time: 35 Cook Time: 5 Total Time: 40 Difficulty: Medium Source: Eglert

Ingredients

6 cups Cabbage

1/4 cup Olive Oil or Grapeseed Oil

4 tbps Balsamic Vinegar

4 tbps Lemon Juice

1 tbpa Salt and Pepper

Directions

Chop Cabbage, put in glass bowl. Salt and Pepper. Mix.
Add oil. Mix again.
Add Balsamic. Mix again.
Add Lemon juice.
